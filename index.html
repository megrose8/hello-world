About Meg

Meg is a Spokane native, Portland transplant. Although she technically only owns Kiwi, she claims Nugget as well. Meg enjoys dressing her dogs up in adorable sweaters, taking them hiking, and perhaps most of all- snuggling with them on the couch.

About Kiwi

Kiwi is a three year old Alaskan Klee Kai, weighing in at a mighty 11lbs. Despite her less than impressive size, she believes herself to rule the entire street we live on and can often be found peering out the windows, patrolling the neighborhood (She's a Karen...) Among her favorite activities are terrorizing neighborhood squirrels, eating trash, and being an absolute snugglebug.

Nugget lives in Nine Mile Falls, Washington and spends her time laying around on the couch and cursing her parents for feeding her cooked squash. She has a skin condition. Besides sleeping, Nugget enjoys eating, hurling her massive body at unsuspecting guests, and visiting Meg in Portland.
